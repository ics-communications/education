About the MA-EL

Scholarly Inquiry Rooted in Faith and Practice

The MA (Phil) in Educational Leadership combines ICS's rich history in integrally Christian scholarship with a commitment to inquiry-based learning. This program inspires and equips educators to create innovative, challenging, and just learning environments that contribute to God's ongoing redemptive work in the world.

Program Structure

45 Credit Hours of Integral Scholarship

The MA-EL is a comprehensive research-focused program consisting of 10 term courses delivered on a rotating schedule. Each course combines asynchronous online learning with biweekly Zoom sessions, plus individualized mentoring throughout.

  • Core courses in Biblical foundations and philosophical worldview
  • Concentration electives aligned with your chosen specialization
  • Praxis & project: sustained original research that contributes new knowledge
  • Individualized mentoring throughout the program from ICS faculty
