About the MWS-E

More Than a Job: A Calling Shaped by Faith, Story, and Wisdom

The MWS-E gives educators a chance to step out of the daily rush and into a community where they can think deeply about their practice and why it matters.

Your Pathway

Two Ways to Complete Your Degree

The MWS-E is designed with flexibility in mind. Choose the pathway that best fits your professional goals and learning style.

Practitioner Pathway

6 Courses + Action Project + Summative Paper

Ideal for educators who want to apply their learning directly in their school context. Complete six courses, carry out a hands-on Action Project in your own setting, and write a summative paper reflecting on the experience.

6 Courses Action Project Summative Paper
Research Pathway

7 Courses + Major Research Paper

Suited for those drawn to deeper scholarly inquiry. Complete seven courses and write a major research paper exploring a topic at the intersection of faith, education, and practice.

7 Courses Major Research Paper

*Students may extend their timeline depending on course load and scheduling needs. Study at a pace that fits your life.

Guiding Principles

Integral Christian Scholarship for Educators

Rooted in the Reformational tradition and shaped by decades of ICS's commitment to integral scholarship, the MWS-E is built on four core commitments:

  • Biblically Informed: Centred on the love embodied by Christ as the foundation for educational practice
  • Deeply Contextualized: Relevant for the real questions and challenges educators face in their schools
  • Aligned with Your Passion: Shaped by your own professional practice and calling
  • Pursuing Wisdom: Through sound scholarly methods grounded in the pursuit of truth
