CSTC

Christian School Teacher's
Certificate

ICS courses approved for the CSTC, administered by Vocate and endorsed by Edvance.

The Christian School Teacher's Certificate (CSTC) is a professional credential that integrates theory and practice in Christian education. It is required by many Edvance-affiliated schools across Canada as a priority for professional development.

The CSTC requires one approved course in each of four areas of study. Several ICS education courses are approved across multiple areas, letting you build toward the CSTC while also earning graduate credit that counts toward the MWS-E or MA-EL.

How It Works

Getting Your CSTC Through ICS

The CSTC is administered by Vocate, not ICS. Here's how to use ICS courses toward your certificate.

Step 1

Register for an ICS education course through the Academic Registrar.

Step 2

Complete the course and receive your transcript from ICS.

Step 3

Submit your CSTC application to Vocate with proof of completion.

Ready to Start?

Contact the ICS Academic Registrar to register for a course, or visit Vocate for full CSTC requirements and application forms.
