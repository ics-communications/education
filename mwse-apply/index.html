What You Will Need

Full Application Checklist

After your inquiry, the Academic Registrar will walk you through each of these components. You may submit items separately.

  • Completed application form
  • Purpose statement: 500–1,000 words explaining how the MWS-E fits with your educational and professional goals
  • Official transcripts sent directly from the issuing institution
  • Two letters of academic or professional reference and one letter of personal reference
  • Academic writing sample or sample of professional work

Questions? Contact the Academic Registrar at academic-registrar@icscanada.edu
